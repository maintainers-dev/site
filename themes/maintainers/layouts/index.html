About

What

maintainers.dev has been built to provide support for open source maintainers, regardless of your experience or type of open source project. This is done through a variety of resources & guidance, in addition to an open forum where you can talk to other maintainers.

Who

This has been started by Dan Brown, an open source developer that found maintainership tricky as their project grew. Experience from growing a project, in addition to taking part in open source communities, indicated that a maintainer-focused resource & community could be helpful to others taking part, or starting out, in maintaining open source.

Why

While there are a lot of resources out there for open source in general, there are few specifically seeking to support maintainers. Upon that much of the existing resources are published, and from the perspective of, large corporate entities that can often over focus on problems not apparent to indie developers, which can be alienating for those starting as an open source maintainer.
